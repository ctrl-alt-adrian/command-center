Surface native currency with approximate USD conversion rather than normalizing to a single currency.

When job listings span multiple currencies (CAD, EUR, GBP, AUD, INR, MXN), display the native currency but provide an approximate USD equivalent for comparison. This avoids incorrect normalization — converting to a single currency risks underselling jobs and losing source context. RoleNext surfaces currency symbol alongside salary ranges and includes approximate USD conversion as secondary information, respecting the source's currency choice.
