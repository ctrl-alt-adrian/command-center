Removing job title, description, and salary from a fit-explanation panel focused it on answering 'why this score' rather than 'what is this job'.

When redesigning the WhyThisPanel for RoleNext's explainable scoring, the user had already clicked into a job and had access to basic job metadata elsewhere. Keeping title, description, and salary in the explanation panel created cognitive load without adding value. The breakthrough was recognizing that the panel's purpose is to answer 'why not higher, why not lower'—not to recap the job. Removing noise let the team dedicate space to what actually mattered: score meaning, matched strengths, specific blockers, and per-pillar explanations. The user was explicit: 'If it does not answer those questions, it is noise.'
