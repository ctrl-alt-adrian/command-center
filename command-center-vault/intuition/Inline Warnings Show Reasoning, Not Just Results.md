Displaying quality warnings inline explains the system's reasoning and builds user trust.

The choice between quietly filtering low-quality suggestions and showing inline warnings (SearchIntentHint component displaying quality assessment) matters for trust. Warnings show users why a suggestion appeared or what's questionable about it, making the system's reasoning visible. Users understand the system isn't arbitrarily gatekeeping suggestions, it's assessing quality. In search, where users often second-guess relevance, showing your work matters.
