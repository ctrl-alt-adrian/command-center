When the solution space is large and context-sensitive, let the LLM choose the specialization instead of hardcoding a static map.

In the interview prep expansion, the specialty category (System Design for SWE, Clinical Scenario for nursing) is chosen by the LLM at generation time based on the candidate's sector. A hardcoded sector-to-category map was considered but rejected. Reasoning: occupations are open-ended, the LLM picks more contextually accurate categories, and the choice adapts per job, not just per sector. Let the model do the work when the solution space is large and requires judgment.
