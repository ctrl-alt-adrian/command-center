Animated drop position placeholders give users clearer feedback about where an item will land than static ring-based drag-over indicators.

RoleNext's kanban redesign replaced a static drag-over ring indicator with an animated placeholder that shows the exact drop position. The ring told users 'something is being dragged over this column,' but the placeholder shows 'your item will land here in this specific position.' With positional feedback, users can predict the final result before releasing. The placeholder uses framer-motion for smooth slide and easing, which also signals responsiveness and reduces perceived lag during the drag operation.
