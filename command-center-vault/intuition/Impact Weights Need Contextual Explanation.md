A numeric weight alone doesn't explain why a gap matters to a user's specific role.

Pure impact weighting tells users a skill gap is important (8/10) without explaining why it matters to their particular job. RoleNext discovered that balancing explainability with brevity required adding contextual language alongside the weight—connecting the gap back to the user's role, not just the score. A number communicates magnitude; explanation communicates relevance. Both are necessary when you're asking users to prioritize.
