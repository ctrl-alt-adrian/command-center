Placing refinement controls inline rather than in a modal keeps context visible and lowers activation energy to iterate.

The LLM refinement card for search intents was placed inline in the suggestion flow rather than behind a modal. Keeping the original query visible while refining it reduces cognitive load and makes users more likely to iterate. A modal would have required navigating away from context, adding friction to what should be a quick refinement action.
