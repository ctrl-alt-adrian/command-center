Visual warnings for weak relevance signals help users understand search result quality.

When search results have weak relevance signals (few matching keywords, sparse match coverage), users might assume the tool is broken or the results are wrong. Low-signal indicators (visual warnings, confidence badges) let users understand why results look sparse — it's not the tool failing, it's the limited match data. This is especially important in interview prep where users are searching for job-specific skills; a weak match indicator prevents frustration and sets expectations.
