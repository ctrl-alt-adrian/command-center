# Instagram

Write an Instagram caption.

Rules:
- Conversational, slightly more casual than LinkedIn
- Open with a hook line (this is what people see before "more")
- 100-200 words
- Add 10-15 relevant hashtags at the end, separated by a blank line
- For technical posts: tell a mini-story with the problem/fix/outcome arc
- For marketing posts: behind-the-scenes tone, like you're talking to a friend about what you're building
