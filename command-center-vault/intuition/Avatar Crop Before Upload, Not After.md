Letting users crop and frame their avatar before upload improves experience by letting them commit to the framing instead of regretting it after.

RoleNext implements avatar upload with a crop/zoom modal appearing before the upload completes, not after. This design lets the user see the framed result and confirm the crop before committing the file. Contrast with post-upload editing: the user uploads, sees the result, and must crop again if it's wrong. Pre-upload crop reduces friction and matches user mental models of framing before committing.
