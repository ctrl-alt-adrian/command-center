# LinkedIn

Write a LinkedIn post.

Rules:
- Open with the specific problem, moment, or realization, not a generic statement
- End with a question or concrete takeaway, not a call to action
- 150-300 words
- No hashtags in the body. Add 3-5 relevant hashtags at the very end, separated by a blank line.
- For technical posts: use the problem/fix/outcome structure but write it as a narrative, not labeled sections
- For marketing posts: tell the story behind the decision or feature
