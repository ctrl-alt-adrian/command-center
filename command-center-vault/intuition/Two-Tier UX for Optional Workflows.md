Show quick warnings inline without forcing a refinement workflow; let users ignore or opt into advanced options.

Not every user wants AI-powered refinement. In RoleNext's intent system, inline quality warnings (small text, icons) appear for all users—no friction. Refinement suggestions are a separate opt-in card below the search box. Users who want help click it; users who are confident ignore it. This two-tier approach reduces friction on features that feel optional or advanced, versus forcing every user through a wizard where conversions drop because casual users feel interrupted.
