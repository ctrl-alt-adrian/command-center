Show quality feedback inline and non-blocking so users can continue searching while receiving guidance.

RoleNext shows search quality warnings inline rather than blocking the user with a modal. This lets quality feedback stream in while the user continues typing. Users can act on the warnings or ignore them without friction. The pattern generalizes: when feedback is helpful but not required, inline and non-blocking feedback reduces friction compared to modal blocking.
