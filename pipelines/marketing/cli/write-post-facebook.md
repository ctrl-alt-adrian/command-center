# Facebook

Write a Facebook post.

Rules:
- Conversational and personal, less formal than LinkedIn
- 100-250 words
- No hashtags. Facebook suppresses hashtagged content.
- Open with a specific moment or observation, not a hook line
- Feel like an update you'd post to friends, not a polished article
- For technical posts: share the problem as a story, explain jargon naturally rather than hiding it
- For marketing posts: behind-the-scenes tone. Talk about a decision you made or a thing you learned.
- End naturally, no hard call-to-action
