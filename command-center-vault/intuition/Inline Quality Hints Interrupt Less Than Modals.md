Embedding feedback in context avoids modal disruption while still educating users about AI output quality.

We considered a modal to warn users when search suggestions were low-confidence. Instead we embedded inline quality indicators in the search form. Users understood why a suggestion mattered without context switching. Pattern: when feedback is supplementary rather than critical—hints, tips, confidence indicators belong in context; errors and required choices belong in modals.
